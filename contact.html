Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campus Calm's service confidential?

Yes, all sessions and information shared with Campus Calm are completely confidential. We adhere to strict privacy policies and professional ethical standards. Your information will not be shared with anyone without your explicit permission, except in cases where there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.

How can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

You can reschedule or cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled time without any penalty. To make changes, simply contact our support team via email or phone, or log into your account on our booking page.

Do I need a referral to use Campus Calm's services?

No, you don't need a referral. Any student can directly book a session or attend our group activities. Our services are designed to be accessible and straightforward to use.

Is there a cost for using Campus Calm services?

For enrolled students, most of our basic services are covered by your student health fees. Specialized services or extended sessions may have additional costs, but these will be clearly communicated before booking.

What should I expect from my first session?

Your first session is typically an intake session where the counselor gets to know you and your needs. You'll discuss what brought you in, your goals, and work together to develop a plan for future sessions if needed. It's a relaxed, judgment-free conversation.
